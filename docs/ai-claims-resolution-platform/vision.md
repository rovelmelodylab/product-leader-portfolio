# AI-Powered Claims Resolution Platform

## Vision

Insurance claims teams spend significant time investigating claim exceptions, reviewing supporting documentation, validating policy coverage, assessing risk indicators, and determining next actions.

Many of these workflows are manual, repetitive, and dependent on specialist expertise.

The AI-Powered Claims Resolution Platform helps claims analysts investigate and resolve claim exceptions faster by combining AI-driven analysis, policy intelligence, risk assessment, and human oversight.

The platform uses specialized AI agents to review claims, analyze supporting documents, assess policy coverage, identify potential risks, and recommend next-best actions.

Human reviewers remain responsible for final decisions, ensuring explainability, governance, auditability, and regulatory compliance.

## Target Outcome

Reduce investigation effort, accelerate resolution times, improve consistency of decisions, and allow claims specialists to focus on complex judgment-intensive work rather than repetitive analysis.
