# AI-Powered Claims Resolution Platform

## Product Overview

An AI-powered decision support platform that helps insurance claims analysts investigate, assess, and resolve claim exceptions while maintaining human oversight and regulatory compliance.

---

## Problem Statement

Claims analysts spend significant time manually reviewing claim exceptions, gathering supporting evidence, interpreting policy coverage, assessing risk indicators, and determining next actions.

These workflows are often repetitive, time-consuming, and difficult to scale while maintaining consistency and auditability.

---

## Target User

### Primary User

Claims Analyst

Responsibilities:

* Review flagged claims
* Investigate coverage issues
* Validate supporting documentation
* Assess claim risk
* Recommend claim disposition

Pain Points:

* Manual document review
* Policy interpretation complexity
* Repetitive investigations
* Large claim backlogs
* Inconsistent decisions

## Goals

### Business Goals

* Reduce claims investigation effort
* Improve analyst productivity
* Reduce claim resolution time
* Improve consistency of decisions

### User Goals

* Faster investigations
* Better visibility into claim risk
* Reduced manual research
* Improved decision confidence

---

## Non-Goals

* Fully autonomous claim approval
* Replacing claims analysts
* Real-time fraud adjudication
* Payment processing
